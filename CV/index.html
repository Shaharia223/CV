Welcome! I am a Research Assistant at the Microgovernance Research Initiative (MGR) and a graduate student in Political Science at the University of Dhaka. I also serve as a Master Trainer at SAVE Youth Bangladesh. My primary interest lies in political data analysis, specifically observing political trends through a data-driven approach. Since 2021, I have been collecting and analyzing monthly data on political violence in Bangladesh, contributing to projects supported by USAID and UKAID. Additionally, I have enhanced my expertise through training sessions from IFES, INTERNEWS, and USAID.

I am passionate about contributing to governance and politics through my work. I have acquired proficiency in data analysis with Python, and data visualization using Google Looker Studio, IBM Cognos, and Python. I am dedicated to fostering improvements in Bangladeshi political discourse and the broader international political domain.
