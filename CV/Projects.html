Action Project: Youthful Democracy 2023

This project was part of my learning in the Democracy from Theory to Practice course. At the end of the course, I worked in a four-member team to implement this action project. The main objective was to implement our learning into action.

We organized a gallery walk and a mock voting event in an open environment at the Dhaka University Campus. The objective was to motivate first-time voters to participate in the Parliamentary Election 2024 in Bangladesh.

Current Projects

PolitixPlume

This is a website which I built on my own from top to bottom in order to fostering a matured political culture in Bangladesh. The main idea here is to collect knowledge from the young and expert, then disseminate it to the greater society. I created this platform to facilitate a rich political culture and equitable as well as quality political education to the young learners.

banglaPolicyAI

In this projects, I am trying to build a system for more effective policy generation. This model aims to include stakeholders' perspectives about a policy to the AI. There will be a model which will collect opinion from the experts as well as the learned stakeholders and give a summary to the formulating body.
